History

The Supremo Barbershop was established in 2017 by JHona Smith, a passionate barber with a vision to redefine the men's grooming experience. From its humble beginnings in a small storefront, Supremo has grown to become a renowned destination for men seeking top-notch grooming services. Over the years, Supremo Barbershop has remained committed to its core values of excellence, quality, and customer satisfaction. With a team of skilled barbers and a welcoming atmosphere, we continue to provide world-class grooming services to our valued clients. As we look back on our journey, we are proud of how far we've come and excited for the future ahead. Join us at Supremo Barbershop and experience the difference for yourself. At Supremo Barbershop, we believe that grooming is not just about looking good; it's about feeling confident and empowered. Our dedication to precision and detail ensures that every client leaves our shop feeling their best. From classic cuts to modern styles, we cater to a diverse range of tastes and preferences. Step into Supremo Barbershop and indulge in a grooming experience like no other. Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we can't wait to welcome you into the Supremo family.
